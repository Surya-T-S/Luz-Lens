About Us

At Luz & Lens, we believe in capturing the magic hidden in everyday moments. Born from a phone camera and a big dream, this space started as a personal photo journal — but quickly evolved into a thriving visual community of storytellers, dreamers, and detail-lovers.

Founded in Government Engineering College, Thrissur, by a student with a passion for photography and storytelling, Luz & Lens is more than just pictures. It's a movement — showcasing raw, unfiltered perspectives and giving every photo a voice.

We welcome submissions from photographers of all levels. Whether you're shooting on a DSLR or a cracked phone screen, if your frame speaks — it belongs here.

So, come join us. Let your lens speak, and let your light (Luz) shine.
